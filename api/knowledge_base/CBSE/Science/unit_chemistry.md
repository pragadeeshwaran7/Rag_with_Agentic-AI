## FAQs on CBSE Class 10 Science Syllabus

### What is the CBSE Class 10 Science Syllabus 2025-26?

The CBSE Class 10 Science Syllabus 2025-26 is the official outline of all the topics and chapters that students need to study in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology during the academic year. It helps students prepare properly for the board exams by showing what to focus on and how marks are distributed.

### What are the main subjects included in the Class 10 Science syllabus 2025-26?

The CBSE syllabus for Class 10 Science includes three main sections:

- Physics – Light, Electricity, Magnetism, etc.

- Chemistry – Chemical Reactions, Acids-Bases, Metals, Carbon compounds

- Biology – Life Processes, Reproduction, Heredity, Environment

Each section helps build basic concepts needed for higher studies.

### Has CBSE released the deleted syllabus of Class 10 CBSE 2025-26 Science?

Yes, CBSE has also mentioned the Science class 10 deleted syllabus 2025-26. Some topics like parts of Evolution and Control & Coordination have been removed to reduce the academic burden. Students should check the latest syllabus to avoid learning removed topics.

### What is the weightage of each unit in the Science syllabus Class 10 CBSE 2025-26?

The syllabus is divided as follows:

- Chemistry – 25 Marks

- Biology – 25 Marks

- Physics – 25 Marks

- Environment – 5 Marks

- Internal Assessment – 20 Marks

This helps students know which chapters carry more marks.

### Are practicals included in the CBSE Class 10 Science 2025-26 syllabus?

Yes, the CBSE Class 10 Science Syllabus 2025-26 includes internal assessments like lab experiments, portfolios, and class tests. These are important for understanding practical concepts and carry 20 marks in total.

### Is there a separate term-wise syllabus for Class 10 Science?

CBSE has returned to the single annual exam format, but some schools may follow a term-based structure internally. If so, the Class 10 Science term 2 syllabus may include chapters like Electricity, Magnetic Effects, and Our Environment.

### Where can I get the latest CBSE Class 10 Science 2025-26 syllabus PDF?

You can download the official syllabus from the CBSE website (cbseacademic.nic.in). Always check the latest version to ensure you’re not studying from outdated material.
