## CBSE Class 10 Science Syllabus 2025-26

Class 10 Science includes chemical reactions, acids, bases and salts, metals and non-metals, carbon compounds, life processes, control and coordination, reproduction, heredity, light, human eye, electricity, magnetic effects of current, natural resources and environment. The syllabus encourages students to observe, experiment and explain natural phenomena scientifically, which is very useful for CBSE board exams and future exams like NEET exam and other competitive examinations.

Students should regularly refer to the NCERT textbooks and Ncert Solutions For Class 10 Science along with this syllabus. ### Internal Assessment (20 Marks)

Internal assessment plays a key role in the Cbse Class 10 Science exam pattern. It includes periodic tests, subject enrichment activities and portfolio work. Together, these components add up to 20 marks, which are added to the 80 marks theory paper.

- Periodic Assessment – 5 marks + 5 marks

- Subject Enrichment (Practical Work) – 05 marks

- Portfolio – 05 marks

Students should maintain a neat and complete Science notebook, perform all experiments given in the NCERT textbook and actively participate in practical work. These efforts directly help in scoring full marks in internal assessment and also strengthen conceptual clarity, which is essential for board examinations and future classes like NCERT textbooks for class 11 and class 12 .
