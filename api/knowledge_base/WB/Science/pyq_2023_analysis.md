# West Bengal (WBBSE) Class 10 Science - Previous Year Questions Reference

This document contains actual patterns and question examples from recent West Bengal Board (Madhyamik) Physical Science and Life Science Examinations. It is used by the AI to ensure the generated questions match the exact textbook-style derivations and straightforward definitions expected by the WBBSE.

## Group A: Multiple Choice Questions (15 Marks)
*Focus: Direct factual recall from the textbook.*
- **PYQ Example 1:** Which of the following gases absorbs long-wave infrared radiation emitted by the earth's surface? (a) N2 (b) O2 (c) CH4 (d) He
- **PYQ Example 2:** The refractive index of a medium is maximum for which color of light? (a) Red (b) Yellow (c) Green (d) Violet

## Group B: Very Short Answer (21 Marks)
*Focus: One-word answers, fill in the blanks, and simple true/false.*
- **PYQ Example 1:** Fill in the blank: The SI unit of resistivity is _____.
- **PYQ Example 2:** Write true or false: The rate of photosynthesis is maximum in green light.
- **PYQ Example 3:** Name the hormone that regulates the amount of sugar in human blood.

## Group C: Short Answer (2 Marks each)
*Focus: Direct definitions, basic differences, and simple textbook phenomena.*
- **PYQ Example 1:** State Charles's Law related to gases.
- **PYQ Example 2:** What is the difference between an alternating current (A.C.) and direct current (D.C.)?
- **PYQ Example 3:** Mention two structural differences between DNA and RNA.

## Group D: Long Answer (3 Marks each)
*Focus: Mathematical derivations, detailed functional descriptions, and standard textbook proofs.*
- **PYQ Example 1:** Establish the relation between the radius of curvature (r) and focal length (f) of a spherical mirror.
- **PYQ Example 2:** Describe the reflex action with the help of a word diagram of the reflex arc.
- **PYQ Example 3:** 10 grams of calcium carbonate reacts completely with dilute hydrochloric acid. Calculate the volume of carbon dioxide gas produced at STP. (C=12, O=16, Ca=40)

---
*Note to AI Validator:* When generating a West Bengal (Madhyamik) Science mock paper, strictly avoid modern, convoluted "case-based" questions. Ensure the phrasing is extremely direct, traditional, and heavily focused on textbook derivations, classic physical laws, and standard stoichiometric calculations.
