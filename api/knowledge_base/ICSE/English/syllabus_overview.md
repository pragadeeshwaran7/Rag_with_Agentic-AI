Candidates will be required to write a composition of about 300– 350 words from a choice of subjects which will test their ability to: organise, describe, narrate, report, explain, persuade or argue, present ideas coherently with accuracy and precision, compare and contrast ideas, and arrive at conclusions, present relevant arguments, and use correct style and format. The topics will be varied and may be suggested by language or by other stimuli such as pictures. The topics will be chosen so as to allow the candidates to draw on first-hand experience or to stimulate their imagination. Question 4

An unseen prose passage of about 500 words will be given. Uncommon items of vocabulary or structure will be avoided. A question will be set to test vocabulary. Candidates will be required to show an understanding of the words/phrases in the context in which they have been used. Several questions requiring short answers will also be asked on the passage. These questions will test the candidates’ ability to comprehend the explicit content and organisation of the passage and to infer information, intention, and attitude from it. The last question will consist of a summary that will test the candidates’ ability to distinguish main ideas from supporting details and to extract salient points to rewrite them in the form of a summary. Candidates will be given a clear indication of what they are to summarise and of the length of the summary.

## How To Download ICSE Class 10 English Syllabus 2025-26?

If you want to download the ICSE Class 10 English Syllabus 2025-26 from the official website that you need to follow the simple procedure given below:

- Step 1: Go to the official website of the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations at cisce.org.

- Step 2: The homepage will open on your screen, where you have to click on the option called Examinations present on the menu bar and then click on ICSE Examinations from the drop-down menu.

- Step 3: Now, click on Regulations and Syllabuses.

- Step 4: Now, click on Regulations and Syllabuses – ICSE 2026. You can download the syllabus for English easily from the new page.

Make sure to complete your syllabus on time so that you can move on to the specimen question papers to revise. The format of the specimen question paper resembles the format of the board question papers, so make sure to go through it completely.
