### ICSE Class 10 Physics Syllabus 2025-26 for Internal Assessment and Project Work

Candidates will be asked to carry out the list of experiments mentioned below:

- Lever - There are many possibilities with a meter rule as a lever with a load (known or unknown) suspended from a point near one end (say left), the lever itself pivoted on a knife edge, use slotted weights suspended from the other (right) side for effort.

- Determine the VR and MA of a given pulley system.

- Trace the course of different rays of light refracting through a rectangular glass slab at different angles of incidence, measure the angles of incidence, refraction and emergence. Also measure the lateral displacement.

- Determine the focal length of a convex lens by (a) the distant object method and (b) using a needle and a plane mirror.

- Determine the focal length of a convex lens by using two pins and formula f = uv/(u+v).

- For a triangular prism, trace the course of rays passing through it, measure angles i1, i2, A and δ.Repeat for four different angles of incidence (say i1=400 , 500, 600 and 700). Verify i1+ i2=A+δ and A = r1 + r2.

- For a ray of light incident normally (i1=0) on one face of a prism, trace course of the ray. Measure the angle δ. Explain briefly. Do this for prisms with A=600, 450 and 900.

- Calculate the specific heat capacity of the material of the given calorimeter, from the temperature readings and masses of cold water, warm water and its mixture taken in the calorimeter.

- Determination of specific heat capacity of a metal by method of mixtures.

- Determination of specific latent heat of ice.

- Using as simple electric circuit, verify Ohm’s law. Draw a graph, and obtain the slope.

- Set up model of household wiring including ring main circuit. Study the function of switches and fuses.

Organize Time - Students can divide the syllabus into small parts and complete them in time. They should prepare a study routine and follow it to complete the syllabus in advance.

## FAQs

ICSE Class 10 Science Paper 2025-26 will be conducted for 80 marks and the duration will be 2 hours. The paper for all subjects will have two sections. Section one will contain short answers. In section 2, students will have to attempt four out of 6 questions. This structure will be followed for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology papers.

To complete the ICSE Class 10 Science Syllabus 2025-26, try to plan out your studies with a systematic study plan. Map out your preparations so that you complete your syllabus at least one month prior to the board exams. Study in small sessions every day.

ICSE Class 10 Biology Syllabus 2025-26 includes chapters like Basic Biology, Plant Physiology, Human Anatomy and Physiology, Population, Human Evolution and Pollution. The exam will be conducted for 80 marks.

ICSE Class 10 Chemistry Syllabus 2025-26 includes chapters like Periodic Properties and Variations of Properties - Physical and Chemical, Chemical Bonding, Study of Acids, Bases and Salts, Analytical Chemistry, Mole Concept and Stoichiometry, Electrolysis, Metallurgy, Study of Compounds and Organic Chemistry.
