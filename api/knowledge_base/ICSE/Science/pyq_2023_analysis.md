# ICSE Class 10 Science - Previous Year Questions (PYQ) Reference

This document contains actual patterns and question examples from recent CISCE Class 10 Science Board Examinations (Physics, Chemistry, Biology). It is used by the AI to ensure the generated questions match the exact cognitive difficulty, precise technical phrasing, and strict Part I/Part II format of the real exam.

## Section A: Compulsory Short Questions (40 Marks)
*Focus: Direct technical definitions, fundamental laws, objective MCQs, and precise anatomical/chemical recall.*
- **PYQ Example (Physics):** State the laws of refraction of light. What is the relation between the refractive index and the velocity of light in a medium?
- **PYQ Example (Chemistry):** Name the gas evolved when dilute hydrochloric acid reacts with zinc sulphide. Write the balanced chemical equation.
- **PYQ Example (Biology):** Define the term 'Guttation'. Which part of the leaf is responsible for this process?

## Section B: Long Answer with Subparts (Attempt 4 out of 6, 40 Marks)
*Focus: Application, detailed physiological processes, complex numericals with mandatory SI units, and labeled diagrams.*
- **PYQ Example (Physics):** 
  (a) A convex lens of focal length 20 cm is placed at a distance of 10 cm from a real object. Calculate the position and nature of the image formed. (3 Marks)
  (b) Draw a ray diagram to show the formation of the image in the above case. (3 Marks)
  (c) Why is a transformer used in the transmission of a.c. power? (4 Marks)
- **PYQ Example (Chemistry):**
  (a) Distinguish between saturated and unsaturated hydrocarbons based on their reaction with bromine water. (3 Marks)
  (b) Identify the functional group present in CH3-CH2-CHO. (2 Marks)
  (c) Explain the extraction of Aluminium using Hall-Heroult's process with a suitable diagram and equations at the electrodes. (5 Marks)
- **PYQ Example (Biology):**
  (a) Draw a neat, fully labeled diagram of the human heart showing its internal structure. (5 Marks)
  (b) Explain the mechanism of double circulation. (3 Marks)
  (c) Differentiate between arteries and veins (any two points). (2 Marks)

---
*Note to AI Validator:* When generating an ICSE Science mock paper, strictly adhere to the highly structured sub-part format (a, b, c) in Section B. Ensure Physics numericals explicitly ask for SI units, Chemistry questions demand balanced equations, and Biology questions emphasize precise terminology and labeled diagrams.
